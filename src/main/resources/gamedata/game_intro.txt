You are in game here your purpose is to reach to your destination.
On your way you will see food, that you can eat. weapons that you can equip.
And, enemies, you have to kill enemies to move ahead of them.

Once you reach your destination you can move to new level.
If you think you are getting killed again and again try searching around for food, weapon.
You can equip maximum one weapon at a time. But don't worry, each time you equip a weapon
attack from previous one will not go away from you.

Disclaimer: The game play may sound rude it is designed like that only.
